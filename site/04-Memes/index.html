Memes

Our primary text for this unit is Limor Shifman’s Memes in Digital Culture, the entirety of which is available as a PDF download from JSTOR: see DOI below. If you want a local copy of the PDF, you will need to download it, which requires registering with EBSCO, which is not difficult but it does add a step, so work ahead of schedule.

Shifman, Limor. 2014. Memes in Digital Culture. MIT Press. DOI.

Blank, Trevor J. 2013. Hybridizing Folk Culture: Toward a Theory of New Media and Vernacular Discourse. Western Folklore 72/2: 105-30. JSTOR.
